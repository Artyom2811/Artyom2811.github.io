Создание прилагательного из глагола

  1. Для описания эмоционального состояния мы используем окончание - ed. Прим: surprised - удивлен.
  2. Для описания отношения к чему-то мы используем окончание - ing. Прим: surprising - удивительный.

Comparison of adjectives

  • Вторая форма - er
  • Третья форма - est

Исключение

  • good - better - the best
  • bad - worse - the worst
  • far – farther, further – farthest, furthest
  • little - less - the least

При добавлении er и est к прилагательным произношение самого прилагательного не меняется. Biggest - бигест.

as + adj + as

  • as + adjective + as.
    Ex: John is as tall as Max; About as bad as expected - примерно так плохо, как ожидалось.
  • not as + adjective + as.
    Ex: My house isn’t as modern as yours.
  • To compare things and show a stronger difference between them, we use a lot, far, much, so much.
    Ex:Tablets are far better than laptops; That dress is much more expensive than the other one.
  • To compare things and show a smaller difference between them, we use a bit, slightly, a little.
    Ex: She's slightly taller than her sister; This task is a little harder than the previous one.
  • We can also use nearly and almost to show the similarity between things.
    David is 70 and Tom is 71. David is almost as old as Tom.
  • We can use not nearly as + adjective + as to show a big difference between things.
    Ex: Mary is 5 and Betty is 20. Mary isn’t nearly as old as Betty.
